Michelangelo

(1475 - 1564)

Most well known for his painting of the Sistine Chapel, Michelangelo was a well-renowed High Renaissance Era painter, architect, sculptor and poet. His artwork, specifically, would influence the many generations to come after him, being considered one of the greatest artists of the Renaissance, and even all time.

Vincent Van Gogh

(1853 - 1890)

Vincent Van Gogh was a Dutch painter, most well known for his work "Starry Night", one of the most famous and recognizable paintings on the planet. Van Gogh is also known for his cutting off of his ear, which he did due to a mental illness that he suffered through his whole life. This led him to eventually committ suicide in 1890.
